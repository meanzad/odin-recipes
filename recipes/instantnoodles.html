Instant Noodles

Description

A Satisfying, Tasty meal on a Budget, hence a Fan-Favourite among broke college students.

Can be prepared in various ways and available in many different flavours, a simple dish that can be elevated into an exciting meal by adding vegetables, eggs and more..

Ingredients

  • A packet of instant noodles
  • Water (250ml)
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large Pot
  2. Add the Noodle bar to the boiling water without breaking
  3. Add the seasoning mix and a pinch of salt
  4. Stir in medium flame for 3 mins
  5. Move the noodles to a bowl without draining
  6. Serve Hot!
