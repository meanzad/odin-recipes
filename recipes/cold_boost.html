Cold Boost

Description

A refreshing and easy to make drink enjoyed by kids and adults alike.

"What's the secret of your energy?"
"Boost is the secret of my energy!

Ingredients

  • Cold Milk (1 Glass)
  • Boost powder (2 Tablespoon)
  • Sugar (1 Tablespoon)
  • Coffee Powder (1 Teaspoon)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Ice (optiional)

Steps

  1. Pour the milk into a large glass
  2. Add the Boost powder and Coffee Powder
  3. Stir well with a spoon and make sure no lumps remain
  4. Add sugar and a pinch of salt
  5. Stir well
  6. Add Ice and enjoy while cold!
