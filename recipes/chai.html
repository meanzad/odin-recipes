Chai

Description

If you're Indian, you already know what it is and chances are that you love it and drink it everyday, if you don't, you're parents definitely do.

For those of you who don't know what Chai is, it's just Tea!

Ingredients

  • Water (1/2 glass)
  • Milk (1/2 glass)
  • Tea powder (1 teaspoon)
  • Sugar (to taste)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a Pot
  2. Add milk to it and wait to Boil in medium heat
  3. Add tea powder
  4. Add Sugar to taste
  5. Enjoy!
